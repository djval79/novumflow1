## üé§ Voiceover Direction Notes

### Voice Characteristics
- **Tone**: Professional yet approachable, confident without being aggressive
- **Pace**: Moderate speed, clear enunciation, natural pauses
- **Energy**: Enthusiastic but controlled, building excitement
- **Accent**: Neutral UK English or clear American English
- **Gender**: Either male or female works - choose based on target audience

### Delivery Guidelines
- **Opening Hook**: Conversational, question-like tone to engage
- **Problem Section**: Slightly frustrated but understanding tone
- **Solution**: Confident and excited, building energy
- **Features**: Clear and informative, highlighting benefits
- **Results**: Authoritative and impressive, stating facts
- **CTA**: Encouraging and motivating, clear call to action

### Technical Specifications
- **Recording Quality**: 48kHz, 24-bit minimum
- **Environment**: Professional studio or treated room
- **Equipment**: Professional microphone (not USB mic)
- **Processing**: Light compression, noise reduction
- **Timing**: Record 10% slower than final - can speed up in post

### Pacing and Pauses
- **Between sentences**: 0.5-1 second pause
- **Section transitions**: 1-2 second pause
- **Emphasis words**: Slight pause before key benefits
- **Call to action**: Slower pace for clarity
