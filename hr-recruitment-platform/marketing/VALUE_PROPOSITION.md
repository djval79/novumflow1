**NovumFlow** is a comprehensive, compliance-first HR and Recruitment platform designed for high-regulation industries (Healthcare, Security, Education). ## üéØ Target Audience
- **Primary:** Healthcare & Social Care Providers, Recruitment Agencies.
- **Secondary:** Security Firms, Education Providers, and any UK-based employer with strict Right-to-Work (RTW) and DBS requirements.
- **Key Stakeholders:** HR Managers, Compliance Officers, Recruitment Consultants, Care Managers.

## üí° Core Value Pillars

### 1. Zero-Compromise Compliance
*Never miss a deadline again.*
- **Automated Daily Audits:** Daily background checks (Cron jobs) scanning for expiring visas, DBS checks, and training certificates.
- **Risk Mitigation:** Instant alerts for non-compliant staff, significantly reducing legal and operational risks.
- **Digital Audit Trail:** Complete history of all checks and document updates.

### 2. AI-Powered Recruitment
*Hire faster, smarter, and fairer.*
- **Smart Screening:** AI algorithms analyze resumes against job descriptions to score and rank candidates instantly.
- **Automated Pipelines:** Custom workflows that move candidates through stages automatically based on scores and actions.
- **Bias Reduction:** Standardized screening criteria to ensure fair hiring practices.

### 3. Unified Workforce Management
*From applicant to active employee in one flow.*
- **Seamless Onboarding:** Convert successful candidates to employees with one click, preserving all compliance data.
- **Attendance & Rostering:** Biometric clock-in/out integration and intuitive shift planning.
- **Employee Self-Service (PWA):** Mobile-optimized app for staff to check rotas, request leave, and view payslips.
