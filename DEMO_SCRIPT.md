## ðŸš€ Demo Flow

### Part 1: The Problem Statement (2 min)

**Talking Points:**
> "Care providers face a fragmented technology landscape. They juggle separate systems for HR, recruitment, compliance, rostering, and care planning. This leads to:
> - Data silos and duplicate entry
> - Compliance gaps
> - Operational inefficiency
> - Poor staff experience"

### Part 2: NovumFlow - HR & Workforce (5 min)

**Navigate to:** https://hr-recruitment-platform.vercel.app

1. **Login & Dashboard**
   - Show the executive dashboard with real-time metrics
   - Highlight: Employee count, compliance rates, pending tasks

2. **Recruitment Pipeline**
   - Navigate to Recruitment
   - Show active job postings
   - Demonstrate application tracking and interview scheduling

3. **Compliance Engine**
   - Navigate to Compliance
   - Show DBS checks, training expiry tracking
   - Highlight automated compliance alerts

4. **Staff Passport**
   - Click on any employee to show their full digital passport
   - Qualifications, certifications, training history all in one place

**Key Message:**
> "NovumFlow ensures you never miss a compliance deadline and always know your workforce status."

### Part 3: Cross-App Navigation (1 min)

**From NovumFlow:**

1. Point out the **"CareFlow"** button in the header
2. Click to demonstrate seamless cross-app navigation
3. Emphasize: "Same tenant context, same user session, seamless experience"

**Key Message:**
> "This is an integrated suite, not separate products stitched together."

### Part 4: CareFlow - Care Operations (7 min)

**Now in CareFlow Dashboard**

1. **Mission Control Dashboard**
   - Show the four stat cards:
     - **Registry Base:** 20 active clients
     - **Deployment Volume:** 26 visits today
     - **System Friction:** 4 incidents to resolve
     - **Operational Units:** 25 staff on-station

2. **Staff Compliance Widget**
   - Show real-time compliance sync from NovumFlow
   - Staff with expiring certifications flagged automatically

3. **Rostering & Scheduling**
   - Navigate to Rostering
   - Show shift management interface
   - Demonstrate creating/assigning visits

4. **Care Planning**
   - Navigate to Care Plans
   - Show a client's care plan with tasks, medications, risk assessments
   - Highlight: Family portal access for transparency

5. **Mobile View (Optional)**
   - Show mobile schedule for carers
   - Check-in/check-out functionality
   - GPS verification and task completion

**Key Message:**
> "CareFlow gives you complete visibility into daily operations while empowering carers in the field."

### Part 5: AI-Powered Features (2 min)

1. **AI Route Optimization**
   - Navigate to Route Optimizer
   - Show how AI optimizes visit sequences to reduce travel time

2. **Incident Analysis**
   - Navigate to Incidents
   - Show AI-powered risk pattern detection

3. **Training Academy**
   - Show AI-generated quizzes for compliance training

**Key Message:**
> "We're using AI not as a buzzword, but to solve real operational challenges."

### Part 6: Integration Story (2 min)

**Explain the Technical Architecture:**

> "Both applications share:
> - **Single Supabase Database:** No data duplication
> - **Unified Authentication:** One login, both platforms
> - **Real-time Sync:** Changes propagate instantly
> - **Shared Tenant Context:** Organization data flows seamlessly"

**Show the Employee Sync:**
> "When you onboard an employee in NovumFlow, they automatically appear in CareFlow ready for scheduling."
